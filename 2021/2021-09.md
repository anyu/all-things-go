## language

- [Artihmetic](https://golang.org/ref/spec#Arithmetic_operators) shift operators
  - `<<` left shift (`x << y` means `x * 2^y`)
    - `1 << i`  = `1 * 2^i`
  - `>>` right shift (`x >> y` means `x * 2^-y`, aka `x รท 2^y`)
  - some use `**` double asterisks to denote 'power of'
